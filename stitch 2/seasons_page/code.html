01. Tonal Texture

The Depth Rule

Autumn styling demands tactile complexity. Pair heavy knits with silk slips in matching tones to create depth without introducing secondary colors.

02. Metal Integrity

The Golden Standard

Prohibit silver and cool-toned hardware. For Autumn Harmony, only brushed gold or copper should touch the skin to maintain the warmth of the palette.
